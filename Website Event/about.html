ABOUT THE EVENT

Xpedition is a thrilling treasure hunt competition where selected teams will set out on an exhilarating mission. There will be an engaging plot that will enthrall every team. Teams will solve the larger mystery as they go along and uncover hidden clues that lead them to new places. The event is scheduled for August 26 at LP 108-109 at 2p.m .There would be two rounds in our event.

Initially, THE PRELIM

An hour-long preliminary round would kick off the competition. The teams would see twenty distinct clips , with unexpected endings. Five thousand bucks would be provided to each team at the beginning. Now, using the options at their disposal, the teams would wager their designated sums to guess the results of those clips and choose their own destiny. At the end of the round, teams will be ranked according to the amount of capital they have accumulated.

Now the main event

There will be eight levels and accordingly these eight clues will be placed at different locations in the college premises. Each team through their provided clue has to reach their designated mark. But it has an unconventional twist. This treasure hunt may not include any riches, but it does contain a bomb. The clues would eventually lead the teams to a bomb where they would have to figure out a way to defuse it, racing against time.
