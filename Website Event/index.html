FLOW OF THE MAIN EVENT

Event Duration:

a.Duration of Treasure Hunt will be 3.5 Hours
b.1 Hour for explaining the rules and discord registration.
c.2.5 Hours for the treasure hunt.
d.Tentative Day and Timings: Sunday,16:00-19:30
Starting Venue: Tan Audi

a.All qualifying teams will gather at the Tan Audi, which serves as the starting point for the treasure hunt.
b.Only 12 teams, each comprising 4 members, will qualify for the treasure hunt.
c.At the starting point, the story will be described to all the teams, and the first clue will be given to all the teams.
Discord Registration:

a.Each qualified team will be registered to a separate discord channel.
b.All Clues will be sent on the discord channel only.
c.A team of 2-3 supervisors will monitor the discord server, sending the next clues to those teams which qualify the previous round.
Eliminations:

a.There will be two checkpoints after the commencement of treasure hunt each at the interval of 45 min.
b.At first checkpoint, teams failing to clear the first two rounds will be eliminated.
c.At Second checkpoint, teams failing to clear the first five rounds will be eliminated.
Levels:

a.There will be 10 levels which have to be cleared for reaching the final level ( Bomb Diffusing Level)
b.There will be four bomb props present at the final location, first four teams reaching the location can collect that props.
c.For clearing the final level, teams have to bring the bomb props back to the Tan audi, there they will get the Arduino Bomb which they will have to diffuse.
Clues:

a.At each level, two clues will be given to a team.
b.A team has to solve both the clues for reaching the next level.
c.One clue is based on location and another linking the story.
Supervisors:

a.At each location, 2 Iste members will be present at some distance.
b.One for verifying the clue and other for keeping a check that any team or any person does not damage the QR code/poster.
c.As a team scans QR code, a message is displayed to them or a message is written on event poster in small font size.
d.That approach the person wearing so and so clothes. As soon as the team approaches that person, he/she will tell the Discord Team to give that team the next clue.
e.Or do some task (Like wave your hand 5 times in air ) , from which supervisor will identify them and will approach them and the next clue is given to that team.

RULEBOOK

1.Teams qualified from the Preliminary round will get into the main event. (8 teams)
2.(Name of the event) is a thrilling ‘diffuse the bomb’ hunt with a duration of 2 hrs.
3.Each team must comprise 3/4 members.
4.Each member of every team will have to register in their respective team discord channel for the clues.
5.Initially, a story will be narrated to all the teams related to which the clues will be sent to them on their respective discord channels.
6.There will be eight levels and accordingly these eight clues will be placed at different locations in the college premises.
7.Each team through their provided clue has to reach their designated mark.
8.Thapar Maps will be provided to each team for accurate navigation.
9.At every location, teams will have to search for a code around the required location.
They have to submit that code in the discord channel, once it gets approved by the team member, the participants will be provided with the further clues.
10.At the last location, the teams will be provided with a prop which they have to bring back to the main venue.
11.To diffuse the bomb, the teams have to enter a code analyzed from all the other codes given.
12.All the participants are required to bring their mobile phones with proper internet connectivity and their own registered discord servers.
13.Trading of clues between teams is strictly prohibited. If found, teams will be disqualified from the hunt.
14.The first team to reach the end point and diffuse the bomb will win the hunt.
