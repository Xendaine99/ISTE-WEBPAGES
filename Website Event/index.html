ABOUT THE EVENT

Xpedition is a thrilling treasure hunt competition where selected teams will set out on an exhilarating mission. There will be an engaging plot that will enthrall every team. Teams will solve the larger mystery as they go along and uncover hidden clues that lead them to new places. The event is scheduled for August 26 at LP 108-109 at 2p.m .There would be two rounds in our event.

An hour-long preliminary round would kick off the competition.In this betting round, teams consisting of three to four players each would battle to make it into the super eight, which would advance to our main event—the treasure hunt. The teams would see twenty distinct clips , with unexpected endings. Five thousand bucks would be provided to each team at the beginning. Now, using the options at their disposal, the teams would wager their designated sums to guess the results of those clips and choose their own destiny. At the end of the round, teams will be ranked according to the amount of capital they have accumulated.

There will be eight levels and accordingly these eight clues will be placed at different locations in the college premises. Each team through their provided clue has to reach their designated mark. But it has an unconventional twist. This treasure hunt may not include any riches, but it does contain a bomb. The clues would eventually lead the teams to a bomb where they would have to figure out a way to defuse it, racing against time.
So, do you have what it takes?! Come find out at our event THE XPEDITION and Unravel the enigma yourself.

RULEBOOK

1.Teams qualified from the Preliminary round will get into the main event. (8 teams)

2.(Name of the event) is a thrilling ‘diffuse the bomb’ hunt with a duration of 2 hrs.

3.Each team must comprise 3/4 members.

4.Each member of every team will have to register in their respective team discord channel for the clues.

5.Initially, a story will be narrated to all the teams related to which the clues will be sent to them on their respective discord channels.

6.There will be eight levels and accordingly these eight clues will be placed at different locations in the college premises.

7.Each team through their provided clue has to reach their designated mark.

8.Thapar Maps will be provided to each team for accurate navigation.

9.At every location, teams will have to search for a code around the required location.
They have to submit that code in the discord channel, once it gets approved by the team member, the participants will be provided with the further clues.

10.At the last location, the teams will be provided with a prop which they have to bring back to the main venue.

11.To diffuse the bomb, the teams have to enter a code analyzed from all the other codes given.

12.All the participants are required to bring their mobile phones with proper internet connectivity and their own registered discord servers.

13.Trading of clues between teams is strictly prohibited. If found, teams will be disqualified from the hunt.

14.The first team to reach the end point and diffuse the bomb will win the hunt.
